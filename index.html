What I teach 辅导内容

NCEA mathematics, Levels 1 to 3. NCEA 数学，1 至 3 级。

Maths is all I teach, so every lesson is mapped to the exact NCEA Achievement Standards being taught at the student's school. The aim is genuine understanding, with proper preparation for both internal and external assessments. 我只专注于数学，因此每节课都紧扣学生学校所教的具体 NCEA 成就标准 (Achievement Standards)。目标是让学生真正理解，并为校内评核 (Internal) 与校外评核 (External) 做好充分准备。

NCEA Level 1 · Year 11

Level 1 Mathematics 1 级数学

Number, algebra, measurement, geometry, graphs and statistics — building the fluency and study habits that Levels 2 and 3 depend on. 数与代数、测量、几何、图像与统计——打好扎实的运算基础与学习习惯，为 2 级和 3 级做好准备。

NCEA Level 2 · Year 12

Level 2 Mathematics 2 级数学

Algebra, coordinate geometry, trigonometry, the foundations of calculus, probability and statistical inference — across both the internal and external Achievement Standards. 代数、坐标几何、三角、微积分基础、概率与统计推断——涵盖校内与校外成就标准。

NCEA Level 3 · Year 13

Level 3 & Calculus 3 级与微积分

The Calculus path — differentiation, integration, complex numbers and trigonometry — with statistics, to the standard needed for first-year university science and engineering. 微积分路径——微分、积分、复数与三角——并涵盖统计，达到大学一年级理科与工程所需的水平。

About Meriel 关于 Meriel

Teaching at universities and hospitals in the UK and Aotearoa. 在英国与新西兰大学及医院的教学经验。

I am a UK-trained medical doctor (BMBS, BMedSci). Over the past two decades I have taught at the University of Cambridge and the University of Otago, and worked clinically across hospital medicine and pathology in the United Kingdom and New Zealand. 本人为英国培训的医学医生 (BMBS, BMedSci)。过去二十年间，曾任教于剑桥大学及奥塔哥大学，并于英国和新西兰从事医院医学及病理学的临床工作。

More recently I have been a Senior Professional Practice Fellow at the University of Otago, tutoring medical students across pathology and clinical modules. I have also led the development of digital learning content for academic and industry partners. 近年来在奥塔哥大学担任高级专业实践研究员 (Senior Professional Practice Fellow)，为医学生讲授病理学及临床模块。亦曾主导多家学术及产业机构的数字学习内容开发。

Maths has run through all of that teaching, and it is what I now tutor. I also wrote the free Textbook of Maths and built the Maths Gauntlet app, so my students learn from resources I have designed from the ground up. With younger students I work patiently and methodically: the objective is for the student to understand the material properly, so that they sit examinations with less anxiety and clearer thinking. 数学贯穿于以上所有教学工作，也是我目前专注辅导的科目。我还亲自撰写了免费的《数学教科书》并开发了 Maths Gauntlet 应用，因此我的学生使用的是我从零设计的学习资源。面对年轻学生时，本人以耐心而有条理的方式教学：目标是让学生真正理解学习内容，从而以更平静的心态和更清晰的思路应对考试。

How lessons work 课程方式

A clear plan, steady pace and NCEA-aligned practice. 清晰的计划、稳健的节奏，并紧扣 NCEA 要求的练习。

Tuition begins with a short conversation about where the student is, what they are aiming for, and where they are finding the work difficult. Each lesson has a written plan, each term has a defined goal, and parents receive a regular progress update. 课程从一段简短交谈开始，了解学生当前的程度、目标，以及在学习上遇到的困难。每节课均有书面计划，每学期设定明确目标，家长定期收到学习进度报告。

01

Free introductory call 免费初步咨询
